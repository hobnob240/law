Do I need to know exact paint colours before asking for a quote?

No. It is enough to explain the job, the rooms or surfaces involved and the type of result you want.

Can I enquire about a small job?

Yes. The form can be used for anything from a single room refresh to a larger decorating project.

Do you cover areas outside Wolverhampton?

Yes, nearby surrounding areas can also be considered. Use the form and include your location or postcode.
